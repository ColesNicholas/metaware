Title
Demand characteristics and full spectrum lighting effects on performance and mood


Developed by
NAC


Checked by
MW


Details
Student
No payment
Not online


Non-control demand levels
Positive: an advertising copy from the manufacturer of the lamps that indicates that full spectrum fluorescent lamps improve performance and mood.
Nil: an FDA paper indicating that claims that full spectrum fluorescent lamps improve performance and mood are highly exaggerated


Procedure
You sit in the room with the lighting


DVs
Reading comprehension test scores: Complete a reading comprehension test
Arithmetic test scores: Complete an arithmetic test
Underlining test scores: Complete a test of how quickly you can underline nouns
Self-reported pleasure: Self-report how positive you feel
